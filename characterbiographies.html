The Story of Samuel De Klerk
Character Biographies

Samuel De Klerk (Sam)

Aliases: The Wizard Of Chaos, Samster, The Demonic One
Abilities: Anything from his book, mainly destructive spells that lay waste to the land.
A hamster wizard... need I explain more?
Personality: Chaotic as hell, loves to cause chaos and break the rules. Once burned down a castle for "the funnies". Quite irrational.
Important Figures: His father (Elder Wizard), Morgan, Aiden (not really), Amelia (the laughing stock), Travis, Harry, Billy, Seth, Alexander, a rock, and Tremaine.

Morgan Silchinger

Aliases: The killer in purple, Morgana, Assassin Of Malice, Purple Guy, William, Man Behind The Slaughter
Abilities: Builds things (like Bob the Builder), likes knifing.
Rancid purple skin, hand shaped for holding a phone, golden badge on dirty purple shirt, black eyes, skinny as a toothpick.
Personality: Murderer, likes killing people but not a terrorist. Tech-savvy, knows how to make nukes.
Important Figures: His parents (if they exist), Sam, Aiden (frenemy), Travis, Billy, Alexander, Seth, Harry, his knife, Tremaine.

Aiden Mcgill

Aliases: The great devourer, the all encompassing void, the bottomless pit, the monster under your house
Abilities: Mass manipulation, weight manipulation, can transfer weight to others, burps that shake mountains.
A whale (smaller for artistic reference).
Personality: Arrogant, prideful, gluttonous.
Important Figures: Ronald McDonald (idol), Sam, Morgan, Travis, Billy, Alexander, Seth, Harry, fast foods, Tremaine.

Travis Leek

Aliases: The Unhinged, dictator of team 0% Censored, decider of children's lives
Abilities: Rockets, terrorism, flight, unhinged, creates digital and physical nukes.
An attack plane (lots of air in his head).
Personality: Like Sam but more extreme, no funny bits, repeats the same points over and over.
Important Figures: Sam, Morgan, Billy, Himself, Alexander, Logan, Seth (His Pookie), Jeremy, Harry, a rock, Tremaine (enemy in war).

Billy Greven

Aliases: The hacker, the physics master, Phys-code, The cube maker
Abilities: Can hack any tech, invade minds, create cubes and balls, control bots he builds.
A computer with a face, body folded in on itself from never touching grass.
Personality: Rage-baiter, main mission is to annoy people, roasts are trash.
Important Figures: Sam, Morgan, Travis, Aiden, Alexander, Logan, Harry, Cameron, Amro, Jude.

Alexander Samway & Logan Bell

Aliases: The schizo's, brain-deads, mad-lads
Abilities: Making others go insane, gaming
Indescribable.
Personality: See aliases.
Important Figures: CELESTE, REWRITE, BENDY (repeatedly).

Summer 'Cameron' Saqib

Aliases: The Harbinger Of Stars, the culling of the day, the night's fear, seed of the cosmos
Abilities: Power over stars and seasons, can summon solar flares, create winters, poison air, control flowers, stars, cosmos, and more.
A sunflower with a face (reference to the eye of providence).
Personality: Can be chill or annoying, loud squeaky voice, likes jokes and speed.
Important Figures: Jack, Michael, Billy, Gab, Jude, Amro, 'Zac', Tremaine.

Amro Hasan

Aliases: Ammo, Arrow, Sorrow, Borrow, lot of o's
Abilities: Power source for others, can create high temperatures to melt metal.
An engine (very, very long).
Personality: A bit dense, a bit smart, level-headed, chill. Collects traits like infinity stones.
Important Figures: Cameron, Jude, Billy, Tremaine.

Tremaine

Aliases: The Inventor, Storymaker, White-Headed Genius
Abilities: Inventor of all stories, can create worlds, characters, and plot twists at will.
A white-headed boy with a buzz cut, always seen with a notepad or gadget in hand.
Personality: Imaginative, clever, always thinking ahead, sometimes mysterious, loves to surprise others with new inventions and stories.
Important Figures: All characters in every story, as he is their creator and guide.
